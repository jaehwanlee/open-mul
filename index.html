Welcome to MuL

MÅ«L (pronounced as mool), is an openflow (SDN) controller. It is developed in C language at its core with python bindings. It has a sophisticated and modern multi-threaded infrastructure. It supports a multi-level north bound interface for supporting applications.

It is designed for performance and reliability which is the need of the hour for deployment in mission-critical networks.

Authors and Contributors

It has been developed by Dipjyoti Saikia who also happens to be co-founder of Kulcloud Corp, a research firm based in South-Korea focusing mainly on SDN and its application in various areas.
